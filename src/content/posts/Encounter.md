*August 15, 1977*

*It is currently 8:30 PM. I'm in the backyard as usual. The sky is clear and the stars are blinking as if the
universe is sending some hidden message. I'm very happy today so I might end the watching early. Jacob and Kathy
are coming for a visit. Jacob's summer vacation has started and he told Kathy that he wanted to meet daddy. Kathy
and I broke up when she was still pregnant with Jacob. So this is the first time I'm going to see my son. Even if
I were to encounter aliens, the joy I would feel would pale in comparison to the joy I'm feeling right now. 
Sometimes I wonder if all this was worth for giving up my life with Kathy and Jacob. Lately I've even begun to 
consider quitting all of this and beg Kathy to take me back.*

*My interest in aliens started when I was in my first year of university. I was neither much interested in 
studies nor was fit or active enough to do sports. I hadn't planned on joining any clubs. That changed after
I went with my friend to visit the astronomy club. He was very much interested in the astronomy and space and
I had just followed him having nothing better to do. Unlike most other clubs at the university, the astronomy
club had no specific purpose. There were a few people who were genuinely interested in the fields of astronomy
but a small portion of the budget was enough for all the things they would want. So the rest of the budget was
used to facilitate the interest of the majority of members of the astronomy club. One night in summer, the club
decided to host a party. Around midnight, after the party was over, a few of us were sitting in an open field, 
looking at the sky. I was looking through a telescope at the direction of Roswell. The clouds in that direction
were moving in a strange direction and I was high enough for it to be interesting. After a moment I saw a shape
come out of the clouds. It was like a metallic object and was moving in a strange way. Suddenly it was out of
sight and I couldn't find it again. Tomorrow morning I recalled the incident and thought that It was just
marijuana's doing. Three days later, I heard the news that some farmer had found some strange looking object
in his sheep pasture. After that my interest and belief in aliens grew more and more each day.*

*Huh! The power went out. There weren't any storms or anything, I wonder what happened. I was thinking of 
wrapping this up but I guess I'll wait until the power is back on.*

*I met when I was working in a newspaper company as editor. Kathy was an investigative reporter. Her stories
were often fascinating and articulated very well. I was in love with her writing even before I met her. After
about a year after meeting her, we started dat- (sound of glass cracking). What was that? I think its my window.*

*It was my window. It had to be now of all time, when the power is out.*

*The glass pieces are everywhere, I have to be careful. There is no sign of anything.*

*There is a puddle of fluid on the floor. I think it blood.*

*I don't think its an animal. There are no furs or anything like that. Also the blood is cold and thin.*

*I'm really scared right now. I can hear some noise. They don't sound like anything I've heard.*
