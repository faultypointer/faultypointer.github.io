The sky seemed to cry last night<br>
like a pure and innocent child<br>
the harrowing cry came to an end<br>
as the last drop traced its descent<br>
splat! it fell on an awry leaf<br>
both came to the ground, leaving the tree with grief<br>
the rain bid farewell with the final wave<br>
and the leaf was shaken yet brave<br>
to take the leaf, the wind had failed<br>
in its place, a white ribbon sailed<br>
after a somber journey, the ribbon took rest<br>
near an empty jacana's nest<br>
and there it sat resting for a while<br>
until the orange rays began to shine<br>
the little jacana picked it up and flew<br>
when he saw a little boy tying his shoe<br>
the boy, in all black, went inside the car<br>
which began to move away and afar<br>
the boy with his father went inside the church<br>
for his mother, his eye began to search<br>
the eyes stopped on a casket, anguish began to grow<br>
and suddenly the choked back tears began to flow<br>
the familiar face, now seemed pale and cold<br>
and still the tears his eyes could not hold<br>
and so the boy bid his mother a final goodbye<br>
tears, too, now falling from the sky<br>
the boy still somber stopped his cry<br>
his last tear drop falling on a leaf that's awry<br>
