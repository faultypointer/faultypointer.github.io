## Breathing
You were breathing unconsciously until you read this sentence. Now you are breathing manually, under conscious
control.  You might be wondering why that is the case. Well let me clear this up, I am not here to explain to
you why or how that happens. As the warning suggests, I'm here to cause you slight distress and building up all 
the way to lead you to experience an eternity of torture. And now you may be wondering why am I doing this then.
Well it's fun, isn't it? So let us begin thi-  back to breathing normally. THINK AGAIN.

## Reading
You are probably reading this text in you mind without producing any sounds. But there is a voice inside you head
speaking every single word you read. Maybe you are noticing this now, probably not for the first time in your
life, after a while. Apparently this phenomenon is called subvocalization. My explanation for whats and whys end
there. I'm here to ask the important questions. Whose voice is it that you hear? For me I actually don't know. I
don't think it is my voice and if it is not mine I don't know whose. Is it some variations of my voice? Is there
actually a voice? Like is the same signal produced when reading aloud is being produced by that voice or is it 
just an illusion? I could spend today searching for answers on internet and I'm sure I would find a satisfactory
answer somewhere. But that not fun. Some questions should be left unanswered. It's okay to not know about every
single fucking thing. Sometimes it is beneficial if you don't know about something at all. Like the game The Game.

## The Game
The objective of The Game is to not think about The Game. Now that you've lost The Game, welcome to the club.
For us losers of The Game, I propose a new game called Not The Game whose objective is to constantly keep
thinking about Not The Game. The rules of Not The Game are as follows: 
1. You lose Not The Game if you haven't thought of Not The Game for the last 2 hours.
2. Rule 1 does not apply if you were sleeping or in general not conscious. In such case, You must thing about Not The Game within 15 minutes of you regaining consciousness.
3. There rule only apply to those who have heard of Not The Game. So anyone who hasn't heard of Not The Game is still a player of Not The Game but are exempt from Rule 1 and 2 until they hear about Not The Game.
